My House Vision

My House Vision

A Drakon where I know you and you know me.

  • Rationale: Other than my OG members, and OGLs, I barely know anyone else from Drakon personally. So to me, when I think of Drakon, I do not have many faces to match to the name, making the idea of Drakon as a house more intangible.I am sure others feel the same way as well, so I hope that members of Drakon can know each other better, in this way making Drakon carry more weight in their hearts.
My Proposed Initiatives

My Proposed Initiatives

Here's some stuff I hope to push out that I think will help in achieving my vision.

The Drakon Chronicles

A notebook shared by everyone in Drakon, write whatever you want in it and pass it someone else random. While you're at it, see what someone else wrote! Once the book is filled it'll be shelved in the library for eveyone to look at.

"Drakon Warrior of the Week"

Every month, a person from drakon will be interviewed for everyone else to find out more about them. Content uploaded to drakon's insta page. For you to know about that person you knew but not really.

Secret Santa

Standard secret santa rules, sign-up based. Alumni from previous years can participate but exchange will take place within respective batches.
